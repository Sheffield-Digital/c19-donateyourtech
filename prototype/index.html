Donate your tech devices

To you it's an old phone thrown in a drawer or your previous laptop that hasn't been switched on in months. To others it the ability to keep up with school work while in isolation or the ability to see their grandchildren via Zoom.

Donate your old technology and make the world of difference to someone during this crisis

Fix and prepare

If you know how to factory reset a phone or re-install an operating system, you could help make sure that the technology that is donated is properly cleaned and ready for someone to use.

Distribute

If you run a community group, food bank or support service, you could help find out what devices woudl most suit the people who need them. Help them work out whether a phone, laptop or tablet would work best and then locate a device for them.

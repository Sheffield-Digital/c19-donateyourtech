Specific info about donating

Your old tech devices might not be the latest version or have be fastest on the market today, but they probably have some use left in them. Even broken technology might be fixable by someone with the right tools and skills.We need you to be honest about the tech that you can donate so that others can work out whether they could make it usable again.

If someone can make use of it, they will contact you and ask you to drop it off (in a safe non-contact way), clean it - both of your data and any bacteria and perform a factory reset and operating system re-install, before passing it on to someone who can make good use of it.
